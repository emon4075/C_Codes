**User Guidelines for Number Guessing Game:**

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game! In this console-based game, your task is to guess a randomly generated number within a certain range. To maximize your chances of winning, follow these guidelines:

1. **Game Objective:**
   Your goal is to guess the mystery number correctly within 5 attempts. The number is randomly generated and falls within the range of 0 to 100.

2. **Starting the Game:**
   - The game will begin by displaying instructions.
   - You'll be asked to enter 'C' in block letters to continue. If you're ready, input 'C' and press Enter.

3. **Guessing the Number:**
   - After confirming your readiness, you'll have 5 chances to guess the number.
   - For each guess, enter a number between 0 and 100 and press Enter.

4. **Hints and Feedback:**
   - Depending on your guess, the game will provide hints to help you get closer to the correct number:
     - If your guess is too low, you'll be informed to guess higher.
     - If your guess is too high, you'll be advised to guess lower.

5. **Remaining Chances:**
   - You'll be notified about the number of chances remaining after each incorrect guess.
   - Keep track of your attempts and make each guess count.

6. **Winning and Losing:**
   - If you correctly guess the mystery number, you'll receive a congratulatory message along with the game creator's information.
   - If you exhaust all 5 chances without guessing correctly, you'll receive a message of encouragement along with the revealed mystery number.

7. **Enjoyment:**
   - Have fun challenging yourself to guess the number!
   - Remember, the game's creator has designed it with care and "Love."

8. **Exiting the Game:**
   - Once you win or run out of chances, the game will end automatically.
   - You can always restart the game if you want to play again.

9. **Thank You:**
   - Thank you for playing the Number Guessing Game!
   - Enjoy the challenge and try to beat the odds.

Remember, every guess brings you closer to uncovering the mystery number. Have a great time playing the game!
